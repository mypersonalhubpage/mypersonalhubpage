About Us

It begins with a passion for the game, we, a group of 6 strive to maintain a healthy lifestyle while having fun playing one of the fastest racquet sport in the world. We aim to squeeze out 2 sessions every week from our busy work schedule as a way to relief the stress and ultimately create time for friendship bonding. On that note, we are looking for more individual who loves the game like we do to join and expand our group connection and network.
Hence, upon reading this and being interested, feel free to reach out, join us for a friendly match and have an update on the schedule.
